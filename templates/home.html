Drowsiness Detection System

Welcome to the Drowsiness Detection System, your digital guardian against heavy vehicle accidents caused by driver fatigue and sleep-related issues. Our web app is a sophisticated solution built to prevent accidents and ensure road safety by detecting signs of drowsiness in drivers.

Utilizing cutting-edge algorithms, our system optimizes eye detection and facial recognition to monitor the driver's condition continuously. The moment it detects signs of drowsiness, such as closed eyes or the face being out of frame, an alarm is triggered, alerting the driver and preventing potential accidents.

At Drowsiness Detection System, we understand the critical importance of road safety and the smooth flow of traffic. Our mission is to provide a necessary tool that prioritizes the well-being of drivers, passengers, and pedestrians alike.

Join us in our commitment to safer roads and a more secure transportation environment. Together, we can make a significant difference in preventing accidents and ensuring a safer journey for everyone.
